Fraud & Disputes

If you believe you have been treated unfairly, help is available.

Unfair treatment by judges or courts

If you feel you have been treated unfairly by a judge or court, you are not alone. Concerns about judicial conduct, procedural irregularities, or bias should be raised with the appropriate oversight authority. If you believe criminal conduct has occurred, the matter can also be referred to law enforcement.

Police or lawyer misconduct

If a police officer or lawyer has treated you wrongly, breached their duty, or acted improperly, you have the right to report it. You can lodge a complaint with the relevant professional body, or pass your concerns on to Crime Stoppers where they can be reviewed and acted upon.

Estate fraud

If you suspect estate fraud, undue influence, a forged will, missing assets, or the misappropriation of an estate, every tip is treated carefully. No concern is too small — the right information can make a real difference.
