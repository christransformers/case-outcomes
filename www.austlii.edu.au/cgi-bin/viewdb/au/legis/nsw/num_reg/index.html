About this Database

This database contains regulations as originally passed by the Parliament of the State of New South Wales. The New South Wales Parliamentary Counsel's Office maintains the official NSW Legislation website which contains current consolidations and historical versions of legislation and legislation as made. All material herein is reproduced by permission, but does not purport to be the official or authorised version.
